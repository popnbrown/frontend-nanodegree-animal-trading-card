Mandrill

No other member in the whole class of mammals is coloured in so extraordinary a manner as the adult male mandrills” — Chales Darwin

  • Scientific Name: Mandrillus sphinx
  • Lifespan: 20 years
  • Height: 1.8 - 2.1 ft
  • Mass: 42 - 82 lbs

The mandrill is a primate of the Old World monkey family. Mandrills mostly live in tropical rainforests. They live in very large groups. Mandrills have an omnivorous diet consisting mostly of fruits and insects
